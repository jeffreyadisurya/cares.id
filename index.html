    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. How do i order cares.id services?

    You can place an order through our application. First you need to register  and get a cares.id account at our online portal. Complete the required data and verify your account. Afterwards you can start to order and browse our services online.

    2. What is the charges for your medical services?

    the price of our services varies depending on what action you need and also what specialization you need. The price rate also depending to our medical partners standards.

    3. Who are your partners?

    We partner with clinics, hospitals, health institution like labs, chemo centre, hemodialysis center and freelance health professionals such as doctors, nurses, midwives and therapists.

    4. What should i do if there is a problem in the online portal or apps?

    You can contact us and report the error or bugs in our platform/apps and we will immediatly fix it and deploy the next app/web version with fixes in place. You can email us an inquiry at : support@cares.id

    5. What do i need to do if help doesn't come?

    Don't panic. You can report back to us at phone no. provided above or email us at support@cares.id and state your urgency so we can help to resolve your problems.
